About Me

I am a full-stack Web Developer

I like to write clean re-usable code that meets indurstry standards, I am passionate about using my creativity and skills to solve problems and challenges.

Ever since at a young age, I have always had a passion for science and technology. I decided to take sciences lessons both in secondary school and at the university. However, with my working experience, I was involved with doing day to day office tasks which made me feel burnt out.I wanted to critically think about problems and use my creativity and skills to solve them.
For the past year,I have spent time learning things I never knew existed and then using my knowledge to build applications I never imagined I could.

I am currently in the Andela ATLP program, a 9 months career accelerator program which was implemented by the Andela and the ministry of ICT.The program aims at equipping Rwandan nationals with the pre-requisite skills to become world-class developers.
